Mission #3 May 25, 1944 42-94758 D/G 4BS Notman ***Feather

Took off at 0511 hours.

Flew as Deputy Lead, position 1-2 in Low Squadron.

Carried 12 500 lb bombs. Did not drop. Brought all bombs

No damage reported.
