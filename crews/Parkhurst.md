Mission #64 September 17, 1944 44-8283 B/G 18BS Parkhurst

Took off from Hardstand #13 at 0602 hours.

Flew position 2-2 in 34 A Group.

Carried 30 260F bombs. Dropped on the Primary at 1012 hours
from 21,000 feet.

No damage reported.

Landed 1126 hours.
